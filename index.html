Tuesday

2/21/1981

Around 9:30AM, calls made to [REDACTED] police department reporting loud bang outside residential neighborhood. Upon arrival, officers noticed smoke plumes outside [REDACTED] family farmhouse. Further inspection into the farm's fields revealed what seemed to be a crash site. Officers on-site reported an unusual smell. Local ATC was contacted by Sheriff [REDACTED] to check if any flights lost contact. I arrived on site at 9:52AM on behalf of the [REDACTED] Defense Agency. The site was closed off to non-agency staff for further investigation.

Around 8:30PM, rattling was heard originating at the crash site. Special Team 22 was assigned to investigate. Check Report 44b for more details.

Wednesday

2/22/1981

The rattling from the previous night persisted into the morning. Special Team 22 was unable to detect any unusual EM activity (Report 44c) radiating from the capsule.

Around 6:30PM, the rattling intensified and a sound was observed alongside the rattling. Special Team 22 observed spikes in EM activity (Report 44d).

Thursday

2/23/1981

At approximately 5:23AM, the rattling and sound abruptly stopped. Special Team 22, however, observed increased EM activity (Report 45a).

At 5:28AM, a creature broke the capsule's exterior and fled. Observers described the creature as black with multiple legs. Special Team 31 was assigned to track the creature.
